CALCULATOR FOR POLYNOMIAL EVALUATION

Enter co-efficients of x from largest degree(power)to lowest degree(power of zero) in first box

Note:enter 0 as coefficient for degrees not present.
Example: .. 2x^4+x^2+1

METHOD

Enter 2 as coefficient of x^4,enter 0 as coeffiecient of x^3 since it is not present and 1 as coefficient of x^2,
then the last 1 as it is a coeffiecent of x to power 0.
click on enter coefficient after each entry and enter value of x in next box,then click on sum polynomial when all is done.
click on display to view coefficients
